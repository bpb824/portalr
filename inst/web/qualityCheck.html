Quality check PORTAL freeway data

Usage

qualityCheck(fData, tolerance = "high", timeCut)

Arguments

fData
Your raw freeway dataframe.
tolerance
A tolerance level of "high", "medium", or "low". "high" is the least conservative tolerance.
timeCut
The time aggregation over which to test quality. Most inuitive strings are accepted, such as "5 min" or "1 hour" or "1 day"

Value

A list of flags seperated by detector and time period.

Description
